The Pioneers of Artificial Intelligence

An AI pioneer is an individual whose foundational work, innovative research, or groundbreaking creations laid the intellectual and technological groundwork for the field of artificial intelligence. These visionaries, from mathematicians to computer scientists, developed the core concepts, algorithms, and philosophies that have shaped modern AI, driving it from a theoretical dream to a transformative reality.

(c. 780 – c. 850)

A Persian scholar whose work in mathematics and logic is fundamental to modern computing and algorithmic thinking.
